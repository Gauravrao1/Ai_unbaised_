# BiasLens AI 3-Minute Demo Script

## 0:00 - 0:25 Problem

"Every day, AI systems make decisions about hiring, loans, admissions, and public services. The problem is that these systems often learn bias from historical data, so they can silently disadvantage women, caste groups, lower-income applicants, or older candidates while still looking accurate on paper."

## 0:25 - 1:05 Product demo

"BiasLens AI solves that with a single dashboard. I upload a CSV dataset or trained model, and the system automatically detects sensitive attributes like gender and caste. It computes fairness metrics such as demographic parity difference, equal opportunity difference, disparate impact, and statistical parity."

"On the right, you can see the fairness scorecard, the protected group comparison chart, and the AI insight panel. The dashboard tells a simple story, for example: women are selected less often than men, or one caste group is receiving fewer positive outcomes."

## 1:05 - 1:45 Mitigation

"The next step is mitigation. BiasLens AI recommends practical fixes like reweighing the data, balancing the dataset, adjusting thresholds, or applying fairness constraints. When I click mitigate, the system shows a before-and-after comparison so we can see whether fairness improved without destroying model performance."

## 1:45 - 2:20 India context

"This matters especially in India, where inequality can show up in hiring, lending, scholarships, and access to opportunities. Our sample dataset demonstrates gender and caste bias in a hiring scenario, which makes the product directly relevant to SDG 10: Reduced Inequalities."

## 2:20 - 2:45 Tech strength

"Technically, this is a real end-to-end system: FastAPI backend, React dashboard, Fairlearn and AIF360 fairness metrics, scikit-learn mitigation, and Gemini-powered explanations that translate math into plain English."

## 2:45 - 3:00 Closing

"BiasLens AI helps teams see bias, explain bias, and reduce bias before deployment. That is how we move from model accuracy alone to responsible, equitable AI."
