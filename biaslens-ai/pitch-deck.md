# BiasLens AI Pitch Deck Outline

## Slide 1 - Title

BiasLens AI: Unbiased AI Decision-Making Dashboard

## Slide 2 - Problem

AI systems are increasingly used in hiring, lending, admissions, and government workflows, but they often reproduce hidden bias from historical data.

## Slide 3 - Why existing tools fail

Most teams look at accuracy only. Fairness is either ignored, buried in technical notebooks, or explained in language non-technical stakeholders cannot use.

## Slide 4 - Solution

BiasLens AI is a dashboard that detects bias, visualizes group disparities, explains the issue in plain English, and suggests mitigation steps.

## Slide 5 - Product demo

Show upload flow, fairness metrics, protected-group charts, AI insight panel, and before/after mitigation comparison.

## Slide 6 - Technical depth

FastAPI, React, Tailwind, Recharts, Fairlearn, AIF360, scikit-learn, Gemini API, and persisted analysis artifacts.

## Slide 7 - India-specific impact

Demonstrate a hiring scenario with gender and caste bias. Connect the product to public accountability and SDG 10.

## Slide 8 - Differentiation

BiasLens AI combines fairness metrics, explanations, mitigation, and an executive-friendly UX in one product.

## Slide 9 - Roadmap

Add model registry support, PDF exports, audit logs, configurable fairness policies, and enterprise integrations.

## Slide 10 - Closing

BiasLens AI helps teams build AI that is not only accurate, but equitable.
